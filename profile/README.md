## What we do

Every Socratic Night is the same format: no lectures, no passive audience.

**AI News Briefing** (first 20 min), a tight rundown of what matters for builders right now.

**Socratic Rounds** (main session), we take community-submitted topics and dig into them together. Questions first, hot takes welcome, receipts required.

**5-Minute Demos**, show what you shipped. No hard selling, just what it does and what you learned.

## How to submit a topic or demo

The [events](https://github.com/Ship-AI-Club/events) repository is where submissions happen.

Open a [new issue](https://github.com/Ship-AI-Club/events/issues/new) in the events repo, titled with your topic or what you're demoing. In the body, say whether it's a discussion topic or a 5-min demo, and drop any relevant links such as a repo, live URL, or screenshots. We pull from open issues to build each week's agenda, so check the [events board](https://github.com/orgs/Ship-AI-Club/projects/1/views/1) to see what's lined up.

No hard selling, no vaporware, if it ships, it speaks.
